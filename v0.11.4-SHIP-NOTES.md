## Why

Week 12 of the 90-day roadmap runs a re-audit against WCAG 2.2 AA. This ship closes the four hardest-blocking audit findings so the re-audit has a clean before/after movement. Total: ~5 engineering-hours, zero new bundle code.

## What shipped

### 1. Light-mode CTA contrast passes AA

Light theme `--amber` darkened from `#C2410C` (3.5:1 on cream `#F5F1EA`, fails 1.4.3 AA) to `#9A3412` (4.8:1, passes). `--amber-2` demoted to old value, `--warn` follows. Every CTA, chip, and accent inherits automatically — no per-component edits. Dark theme untouched (already 7.8:1).

### 2. `<h1>` on the three pages that were missing one

Audit claimed "zero `<h1>` on any page" — actually true only for Home, NBADashboard, HomeV1. The other 17 pages already shipped h1. Added visually-hidden `<h1 className="sr-only">` to the three holdouts:

- Home: "gibol.co — dashboard olahraga live dalam Bahasa Indonesia"
- NBADashboard: "Skor Live NBA Playoff 2026 — Bracket, Peluang Juara, Play-by-Play"
- HomeV1: same as Home

Hidden from desktop visuals (dashboard cards carry the brand beat already), fully announced to VoiceOver/JAWS so the heading rotor works. WCAG 1.3.1 + 2.4.6.

### 3. Skip-to-content link

First focusable element in App.jsx, above `V2TopBar`. Visible only on focus; slides down over the masthead in amber-bordered pill. Jumps to `#main` so keyboard users reach the body in one keystroke instead of ~40 tabs through sport nav. Copy: "Langsung ke konten · Skip to content." WCAG 2.4.1.

### 4. `<main id="main">` landmark

`<main id="main" tabIndex={-1}>` wraps `<Suspense>` in App.jsx. One edit gives every route a `<main>` landmark without touching individual pages. Fixes Pak Haryanto's VoiceOver rotor-landmarks "no items." WCAG 1.3.1 + 4.1.2.

### 5. DayScoreboard + EPLDayScoreboard tap targets

Date-tab tiles:

- **Before:** ~38×34 px with 9.5 / 11 / 8.5 px labels. Fails WCAG 2.5.8 AA (24×24 min).
- **After:** 56+ px min-height, 96+ px min-width, 11 / 13 / 11 px labels. Passes enhanced 2.5.5 AAA (24×24) and meets the 44×44 recommendation for thumb ergonomics.

Also added `aria-current="date"` on the active tab and descriptive `aria-label` per tile ("SEN 22/04 — 4 laga, ada live"). Same treatment applied to both scoreboards so NBA and EPL feel consistent.

## What's NOT in this ship (deferred on purpose)

- `aria-live="polite"` on the score feed. Audit listed it as Sprint 1; I pushed back — naive implementation spams screen readers on every 10–30s poll. Needs a throttled announcer + "significant event" filter. Reslotted to Sprint 3 (~2 days).
- Inactive chip border contrast (1.4.11). Token-only change; batching with Sprint 1 week-2 along with custom-chip `role="button"` + `aria-pressed`.
- Performance stagger / skeletons / SW extension. That's Sprint 2 scope.
