## What shipped

### 1. Dashboard `/premier-league-2025-26`

Replaces the coming-soon stub with a real Bahasa-first dashboard, 4 panels:

- **Klasemen** — 20 clubs, rank/points/W-D-L/GF/GA/GD/form. Qualification zones (UCL 1-4 blue, UEL 5 amber, UECL 6 green, Rel 18-20 red) tint the row at ~5% alpha. Legend row explains zones. Bahasa form letters M/S/K (Menang/Seri/Kalah); English toggles to W/D/L.
- **Jadwal** — upcoming fixtures (next 7 days) via ESPN scoreboard, kickoff WIB.
- **Hasil** — last 7 days of completed fixtures, result colour-coded for winner.
- **Top skor** — Golden Boot top 10 from ESPN leaders endpoint, each chip team-coloured.
- **Indeks Klub** — auto-fill grid linking to all 20 per-club pages (internal link graph).

Hero spec matches Step 6 revamp: Space Grotesk 36 / weight 700 / letter-spacing -0.025em / line-height 1.05 / `textWrap: balance`. Accent tint ≤14% using PL purple `#37003C`. Chip top-right shows `LIVE`.

### 2. 20 per-club SEO pages `/premier-league-2025-26/club/:slug`

Each club indexable with its own `SportsTeam` JSON-LD. Sections:

- **Hero** — name, stadium, city, founded.
- **Klasemen + Form** — current rank, points, P/W/D/L/GD, goals for/against, last-5 form pills, qualification zone badge if in one.
- **Jadwal** — next 5 fixtures, `KANDANG`/`TANDANG` badge, opponent link.
- **Hasil** — last 5 completed fixtures, M/S/K badge + score.
- **Top skor klub** — any of this club's players in the league top 10 (filtered from `useEPLScorers`).
- **Tentang** — Bahasa paragraph from `clubs.js::bio`.

Slugs: `arsenal, aston-villa, bournemouth, brentford, brighton, burnley, chelsea, crystal-palace, everton, fulham, leeds, liverpool, manchester-city, manchester-united, newcastle, nottingham-forest, sunderland, tottenham, west-ham, wolves`.

### 3. Data layer

Four new hooks wrapping ESPN soccer/eng.1 through `/api/proxy/*`:

| Hook | Endpoint | Cache | Polling |
|---|---|---|---|
| `useEPLStandings` | `espn-v2/soccer/eng.1/standings?season=2025` | s-maxage=60 | 60s |
| `useEPLFixtures` | `espn/soccer/eng.1/scoreboard?dates=YYYYMMDD-YYYYMMDD` | s-maxage=60 | 60s |
| `useEPLScorers` | `espn-common/soccer/eng.1/leaders?limit=20` | s-maxage=300 | 5min |
| `useEPLTeam` | `espn/soccer/eng.1/teams/{id}` + `/teams/{id}/schedule` | s-maxage=60 | one-shot |

`api/proxy.js` gained two providers:

- `espn-v2` → `https://site.api.espn.com/apis/v2/sports` (60s cache)
- `espn-common` → `https://site.web.api.espn.com/apis/common/v3/sports` (300s cache)

### 4. SEO surface

- `sitemap.xml` — 20 new club URLs at priority 0.6, `/premier-league-2025-26` bumped from 0.7 → 0.9.
- `adapter.prerenderRoutes()` — 21 routes (1 dashboard + 20 clubs). Each emits its own JSON-LD (`SportsEvent` for the dashboard, `SportsTeam` for each club).
- Home card flipped: `status: 'soon'` → `'live'`, tag `COMING SOON` → `LIVE`, CTA `Segera Hadir` → `Masuk →`, blurb rewritten.
