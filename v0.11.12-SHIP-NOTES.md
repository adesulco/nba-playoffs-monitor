# v0.11.12 — cross-dashboard audit fixes

**Date:** 2026-04-23
**Branch:** main
**Risk:** LOW. A11y + heading-hierarchy fixes only. No layout or data-path change; three defects are pure tag changes, one is a space-in-JSX, one is a conditional className.
**Audit ref:** `docs/audits/2026-04-23-live-sport-dashboard-audit.md`

---

## Why

The live audit (Claude-in-Chrome MCP sweep of NBA + EPL + F1 + Tennis at 1440 px) surfaced five discrete defects that slipped past Sprint 1's a11y floor and Sprint 5's editorial pass. All are small but together they fix the "dashboards feel like different products" perception. No design decisions required — these are bugs.

---

## What shipped

### 1. F1 hub now has an `<h1>`

`src/pages/F1.jsx` — the "Formula 1 2026" title `<div>` promoted to `<h1>`. Keeps identical visual style (Inter Tight 36 px 700). Sprint 1 added h1s to Home / NBADashboard / HomeV1 but missed F1 and Tennis hubs.

### 2. Tennis hub now has an `<h1>`

`src/pages/Tennis.jsx` — same treatment on the "Tennis 2026" title.

### 3. NBA's double-`<h1>` fixed

`src/components/SEOContent.jsx` — the "2026 NBA Playoffs — Live Scores & Companion Dashboard" heading was a second `<h1>` on the page, fighting the visually-hidden sr-only h1 I added in v0.11.4. Demoted to `<h2>`. Structured-data JSON-LD is unchanged (it lives in the separate `<Helmet>` block). WCAG 1.3.1 violation gone.

### 4. Tennis h2 / h3 hierarchy inversion

`src/pages/Tennis.jsx` — "Active & upcoming tournaments" h2 raised 14 → 15 px (matches EPL/F1 panel baseline).
`src/components/tennis/TournamentCard.jsx` — child heading demoted `<h3>` 16 px → `<h4>` 14 px so parents are always larger than children. No visual squish; compact list rhythm preserved.

### 5. F1 "Drivers Standings 2026" text-concat bug

`src/pages/F1.jsx` — the `<h2>` rendered season year in a child `<span>` with `margin-left: 8`. Visually fine; `textContent` flattened to `"Drivers Standings2026"` (no space), which is what screen readers and analytics heading-extractors consume. Added a literal space node between the two; visual gap preserved by the span's margin.

### 6. Signature tick device on Tennis live ribbon

`src/pages/Tennis.jsx` — "Active & upcoming tournaments" h2 now carries `.tick-live` className whenever any tournament in the ribbon has `status === 'live'`. Closes the v0.11.11 gap where Madrid Open was LIVE but the header had no signature marker.

### 7. `.panel-title-mono` utility class (reserved, not wired)

`src/index.css` — new utility:

```css
.panel-title-mono {
  font-family: var(--font-mono);
  font-size: 12px;
  font-weight: 700;
  letter-spacing: 1.5px;
  text-transform: uppercase;
  color: var(--ink);
  margin: 0;
}
```

Shipped but not applied — it exists for the follow-up ship that unifies EPL/F1/Tennis panel titles with NBA's 10 px Bloomberg-terminal voice. The decision of whether to adopt (and whether to go 10/12/15 px) deserves a designed before/after, not a speculative refactor.

## What's deliberately deferred

- **NBA content-area top-offset alignment with EPL/F1/Tennis.** NBA intentionally runs dense from line 1 (YesterdayRecap is an optional collapsible strip; below it is DayScoreboard). EPL/F1/Tennis all have a marketing-hero at the top with 24 px padding. Forcing NBA into the same shape hurts density more than it helps alignment. Revisit after a design pass.
- **Panel-title voice unification** (see #7 above).
- **LiveHero parity on Home for EPL / Tennis.** NBA-only today; the audit noted this gap. Separate ship.
