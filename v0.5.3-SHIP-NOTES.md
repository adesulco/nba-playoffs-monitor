## What shipped

### 1. Live match spotlight (Sedang main)

New `LiveSpotlight` section on `/premier-league-2025-26`. When any EPL match is currently in-progress, a prominent card appears at the top of the dashboard showing:

- LIVE pulse + match clock (e.g. `67'`)
- Team names, accents, live score in mono 18px
- Prominent SHARE button per match

When no matches are live, the section renders nothing ‚Äî zero dead chrome on off-days.

Implementation: [`useEPLFixtures`](src/hooks/useEPLFixtures.js) already tracks `statusState` (`'pre' | 'in' | 'post'`). In [`EPL.jsx`](src/pages/EPL.jsx), `upcoming[]` is split via `useMemo` into `live[]` (ESPN `in` state) and `scheduled[]`. `LiveSpotlight` renders `live`; `Jadwal` renders `scheduled` (so a live match never appears in two sections).

### 2. ShareButton on every fixture row

- **LiveSpotlight** ‚Äî "ARS 2 ‚Äì 1 MCI ¬∑ 67' ¬∑ live-update-nya di gibol.co ‚öΩ"
- **Jadwal** ‚Äî "Arsenal vs Man City ¬∑ Sab 26 Apr ¬∑ 21:00 WIB ¬∑ jadwal di gibol.co ‚öΩ"
- **Hasil** ‚Äî "FINAL ¬∑ ARS 2 ‚Äì 1 MCI ¬∑ recap di gibol.co ‚öΩ"

Cascading share: `navigator.share` (mobile native sheet) ‚Üí popover with WhatsApp ¬∑ X ¬∑ Copy. Analytics events: `epl_share_live`, `epl_share_upcoming`, `epl_share_final` (channel breakdown auto-tagged by [`ShareButton`](src/components/ShareButton.jsx)).

Per-match URL today: `https://www.gibol.co/premier-league-2025-26#{matchId}`. Will be a real match-detail page in a future phase.

### 3. Fangir partner slot on EPL

`<FangirBanner />` rendered between the data and the disclaimer on EPL. Same component NBA ships (the v0.5.2 pack variant at Rp 37.500). Partner funnel now runs off the EPL window too ‚Äî 380 PL matches/season is a much larger surface than the ~80 NBA playoff games.
