## What shipped

- **GIB-013** — HomeV1 `<CardHead>` title promoted from `<span>` to `<h2>` in `src/components/v2/Card.jsx`. HomeV1 went from 0 h2s on homepage to ~6. Same visual style (10 px uppercase mono eyebrow, 0.14em letter-spacing).
- **GIB-003** — `ComingSoon.jsx` page title promoted `<div>` to `<h1>`. One edit covers FIFA World Cup (`/fifa-world-cup-2026`), Liga 1 (`/liga-1-2026`), and IBL (`/ibl`). All three "coming soon" hubs now have a heading.
- **GIB-014** — `<footer role="contentinfo">` on every main page: Home, NBADashboard, EPL, F1, Tennis, ComingSoon, Recap. `ContactBar` stays inline as a child; landmark rotor now lists a footer on every hub.
- **GIB-015** — new `<ScoreAnnouncer />` at `src/components/ScoreAnnouncer.jsx`. A visually-hidden `aria-live="polite" role="status"` region. Parent-driven — `NBADashboard` tracks `prev-status` per game via `useRef` and only announces on `pre → in` (tip-off) or `in → post` (final). No per-poll chatter. EPL + Tennis follow same pattern in a later ship.
- **Bonus chip-contrast fix** (surfaced by Day 1 axe regression):
  - `--live` / `--down` tokens darkened `#EF4444 → #F25757`. Contrast on `--bg-2` jumps from 4.43 : 1 (just under AA) to **5.24 : 1** (clears AA). Red-500 feel preserved.
  - `Chip variant="live"` no longer respects `sportId`. Previously `<Chip variant="live" sportId="tennis">` rendered **white text on light-gold (1.67 : 1)** — the single worst contrast violation on site (per audit Tennis `#ffffff on #e6c47a` finding). Now "live" always uses `--live`. Sport branding stays on the `soon` and `neutral` variants.
